Note - Graphics > Page 150
Another important reason was that some compilers/linkers only supported a maximum of 7 letters for exported symbols. One other reason is that seeing short names everywhere (e.g: one letter command-line options) is very contagious. Another reason is that most programmers (especially who aren't exposed to many different codebases) don't appreciate how expensive is unnecessary implicit understanding of the code, have never seen high-quality code in their life, or have high tolerance for accidental complexity.
